Hello there! I am a junior visual designer currently working at Sparkt.

My journey started in 2018 as a content writing intern at Vogue India. After that I started my undergrad in engineering and soon enough, was obsessed with coding, and web design. This led me to where I am at right now, Sparkt. I started out as a junior web designer and then gradually, in the second half of 2019, moved into visual direction.

I have a naturalistic, minimal style and always looking for the small moments and big truths. I love working with people and brands who want to tell stories that inspire us to be better.
